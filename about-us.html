ARCHIVING THE UNRELEASED

The minds and the research behind the digital preservation of vaporware projects.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Vaporware is a digital archive dedicated to cancelled video games and unreleased interactive projects.

The website combines historical research with original 3D objects inspired by iconic vaporware titles, preserving fragments of unfinished gaming history.

ABOUT US

Vaporware was created by two students passionate about video game history, digital preservation, and 3D design.

The project was developed as a combination of archival research, web design, and low-poly 3D modeling inspired by unreleased games and forgotten prototypes.
