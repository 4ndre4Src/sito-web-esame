HISTORICAL & TEXTUAL SOURCES

ARCHIVAL DATABASES

  • Games That Weren't: gamesthatwerent.com — An invaluable archive for cancelled, beta, and unseen video games.
  • The Cutting Room Floor: tcrf.net — Dedicated to unearthing and documenting unused and cut content in video games.
  • Sega Retro: segaretro.org — Historical documentation, logs, and technical press releases regarding the Sega Neptune.
  • Kirby Fandom: kirby.fandom.com/ — the passionate community of fans who love Nintendo’s iconic Kirby video game series.

ARTICLES & DOCS

  • Nintendo PlayStation: polygon.com — Investigative articles and interviews regarding the discovery of the SFX-100 prototype.
  • Atari Panther Logs: atarimuseum.com — Original technical specifications and internal hardware development history from Atari.
  • Cyberpunk 2077 E3: cyberpunk.net — Reports on the development cycle, broken promises, and internal management of the 2018 build.
MEDIA & VIDEO REFERENCES

VIDEO ESSAYS & FOOTAGE

  • Crowbcat: youtube.com/crowbcat — Analysis videos and direct comparisons between downgrades and initial presentation showcases.
  • Digital Foundry: youtube.com/digitalfoundry — Technical performance breakdowns, frame-rate analyses, and asset comparison logs.
  • Gaming Historian: youtube.com/gaminghistorian — The Gaming Historian is a documentary series all about the history of video games.

ASSETS & TOOLS

  • Sketchfab Community: sketchfab.com — Reference frames and interactive material topologies for retro-console geometry reconstruction.
  • Game UI Database: gameuidatabase.com — Layout references for industrial, glitch, and cybernetic aesthetic design patterns.
  • Game Storm: gamestorm.com — A prominent Italian gaming portal that provides news, reviews, cheats, and hosts an active community for various console and PC games.
